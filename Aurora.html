Aurora threads boutique

Project Overview

A comprehensive dashboard for Aurora Threads Boutique, providing insights into sales and customer metrics across 3 stores from 771 transactions.

Key Features:

  • Displays total revenue ($288K) and quantity sold (771).
  • Breaks down revenue by product category, day, gender, and payment type.
  • Lists customer-specific revenue and quantity sold data.
